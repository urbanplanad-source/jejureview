Beginner's Guide to Skin Laser Treatments in Jeju

March 1, 2026

Pigmentation Lasers (Spot Removal) — Requires Multiple Sessions

Target freckles, sun spots, and uneven skin tone. Laser Toning is the most common option, but visible results typically require multiple sessions. A single-visit treatment won't complete a full course—ask your clinic about 'single-session expectations' before booking.

Pores / Acne Marks / Scars — Longer Downtime

Primarily treated with fractional lasers (Fraxel-type) or Potenza RF microneedling. Downtime is approximately 3–7 days. Factor this into your travel schedule and plan these treatments towards the end of your trip.

Skin Elasticity / Hydration — Best for Travellers

Rejuran (polynucleotide injection) and skin booster injections offer gentle effects with minimal downtime. Well-suited to a single in-trip treatment without disrupting your itinerary.

Important: Sun Protection Is Essential

All laser treatments require strict sun protection afterwards. Jeju receives strong UV radiation year-round, so apply SPF50+ sunscreen diligently. Plan your laser treatment towards the end of your trip or just before departure to minimise post-treatment sun exposure.
