Thermage FLX & Ulthera Lifting: What Travellers Need to Know

March 8, 2026

Effect Timeline: Why "Nothing Happened Immediately" Is Normal

Lifting treatments are among the most popular procedures for medical tourists visiting Jeju. Many travellers wonder "why don't I feel any difference?"—and the answer is simple.

Lifting results typically emerge gradually over 1–3 months after treatment. Not seeing immediate dramatic change during your trip is completely normal—it does not mean the treatment failed.

Why Individual Results Vary So Much

Results can vary 2–3× depending on age, degree of skin laxity, and lifestyle. Other people's experiences in reviews are a reference only and cannot guarantee the same outcome for you.

In particular, people under 35 with good skin elasticity may feel less immediate effect than older patients—this is also normal.

Post-Treatment Care

Avoid intense exercise and saunas on the day of treatment. Mild redness or slight swelling for 2–4 weeks afterwards is a normal response and should not cause concern.

How to Read Reviews Correctly

When reading reviews, distinguish between 'how it felt immediately after' and 'how it looked 1–2 months later'. The former reflects the in-clinic sensation; the latter reflects the actual lifting result. Many travellers miss this distinction.
